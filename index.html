⬡ How to Use APEX
🚀 Quick Start
1️⃣
The site opens on Market Picks — a live-scored watchlist of stocks ranked by technical strength right now.
2️⃣
Click any stock card to jump straight to its full chart with all indicators. Hit the browser Back button to return to Market Picks.
3️⃣
Use the search bar at the top — type any ticker (AAPL) or company name (Apple) and press Enter.
4️⃣
The Market Pulse bar (SPY / QQQ / DIA / IWM / VIX) is clickable — tap any index to open its chart.

📊

💡
Hover the score circle to see exactly why — RSI level, MACD crossover status, trend relative to MA20/MA50, 5-day momentum, volume spike, and Bollinger band position, all explained in plain English.
📈
AI Top Picks — 20 large & mid cap stocks sorted by score. Highest conviction opportunities listed first.
◎
Early Entry Radar — 20 smaller, cheaper stocks showing unusual momentum (PLTR, IONQ, RKLB, etc.). Higher risk, higher potential. Good for spotting moves early.
▦
Sector Trends — 20 ETF scores showing where market money is rotating (XLK, XLF, XLE, GLD, TLT, ARKK, and more).
↻
Data auto-refreshes every 90 seconds when the market is open. Use the Refresh button to update manually anytime.
🧠 How Scores Are Calculated
📊
RSI (±18 pts) — Relative Strength Index. RSI below 30 (oversold bounce) or above 70 (strong momentum) both score well. Extreme RSI in the right direction = max points.
⚡
MACD (±26 pts) — Most heavily weighted factor. A recent bullish crossover (MACD line crossing above signal) adds up to +18 pts. MACD above zero adds another +8 pts.
〰️
Moving Averages (±16 pts) — Price above MA20 = +8 pts. Price above MA50 = +8 pts. Confirms the stock is in an uptrend on multiple timeframes.
📈
5-Day Momentum (±10 pts) — How much the price has moved over the last 5 trading days. Captures short-term directional conviction.
🔥
Volume Spike (±8 pts) — Today's volume vs 20-day average. 2× normal volume with a rising price = institutions buying = +8 pts. High volume on a down day = −8 pts.
📏
Bollinger Bands (±10 pts) — Price breaking above the upper band signals a strong breakout (+10). Price sitting below the lower band signals oversold conditions (+8 for a potential bounce).
📡
The same scoring engine powers both Market Picks and the Chart view signal card — scores always match.

📐 Trade Setup Panel
🎯
Entry — suggested price to enter, based on current price and nearby support/resistance levels.
🛑
Stop Loss — the exit price to limit your loss. Calculated from ATR (Average True Range) — the stock's normal daily volatility.
✅
TP1 / TP2 / TP3 — three take-profit targets at increasing distances. TP1 is conservative, TP3 is ambitious.
⚖️
R/R Ratio — Risk/Reward. A 1:3 ratio means for every $1 risked, potential gain is $3. Look for at least 1:2.
⚠️
These levels are auto-calculated from technical data — not personalized financial advice.
📉 Indicators Explained
〰️
MA20 / MA50 / MA200 — Simple moving averages. Price above MA = uptrend. Price crossing MA from below = potential buy signal. MA200 separates bull and bear market territory.
〰️
EMA9 — Exponential moving average, reacts faster to recent price. Good for catching short-term trend changes before MAs catch up.
📏
Bollinger Bands — Volatility bands 2 standard deviations from MA20. Price near upper band = potentially overbought. Near lower band = potentially oversold.
⚓
VWAP — Volume Weighted Average Price. Institutions use this as a fair-value benchmark. Price above VWAP = bullish intraday. Best on 1D and intraday ranges.
📊
RSI (14) — Relative Strength Index. Above 70 = overbought (watch for pullback). Below 30 = oversold (watch for bounce). 50 = neutral momentum.
⚡
MACD — Trend-momentum indicator. MACD line crossing above signal line = bullish. Crossing below = bearish. Histogram bars show the strength of the move.
